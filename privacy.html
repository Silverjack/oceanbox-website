Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Oceanbox Logistic Limited collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you visit our website, submit a quote request, request inventory information, or receive commercial email communications from us.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect business and contact information you provide directly to us, including your name, company name, business email address, phone number, shipping or depot requirements, request details, and any information you include in your messages or quote inquiries.

2. Information Collected Automatically

We may collect limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, referral information, and anti-spam verification signals when you interact with our website. We use this information to secure, operate, and improve our services.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to respond to inquiries, prepare quotations, send inventory sheets, manage customer and prospect relationships, improve our website, detect abuse or fraud, provide business communications, and send marketing emails, commercial updates, and product availability notices that may be relevant to your business.

4. Email Marketing and Opt-Out

If you request information from Oceanbox or otherwise provide your business contact information, we may send you marketing emails and inventory updates related to our container trading, leasing, and logistics services. You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link included in the email or by contacting us at rolly@oceanbox.cn. We may still send non-marketing service communications where necessary to respond to an active request or business transaction.

5. Sharing of Information

We may share information with service providers that support website hosting, CRM and lead management, email delivery, anti-spam verification, analytics, and operational support. We may also disclose information when required by law, to protect our rights, or in connection with a business transfer or restructuring.

6. Data Retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary to manage customer and prospect relationships, maintain records of commercial activity, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and improve our services.

7. International Data Transfers

Oceanbox may process or store information in countries other than the country where you are located. By using our website or submitting information to us, you understand that your information may be transferred across borders in connection with our normal business operations and service providers.

8. Your Choices and Rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, or object to or limit certain processing activities. To make a request, contact rolly@oceanbox.cn. We may need to verify your identity before completing a request.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Our website and service providers may use cookies or similar technologies to support website functionality, security, analytics, and form protection. You can control cookie settings through your browser, though some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The revised version will be posted on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the website or continued receipt of our communications after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact rolly@oceanbox.cn.
