How We Execute

Choose the Transaction Model That Fits Your Operation

Pickup (3-Step Process)

Step 1: Send your requirement and confirm key specs (location, quantity, unit type, and condition).
Step 2: Receive a clear commercial offer and invoice.
Step 3: Complete payment and get release for pickup.

Speed is a core advantage of this model. Most pickup releases are completed within one business day, and many can be released the same day after payment confirmation.

Gatebuy

For gatebuy, the empty container is returned directly to the yard your team specifies. We align return location, handover details, and reference flow in advance, so the transaction closes cleanly and is easy to audit.

Production Order (China to Destination)

If your requirement is not available in live stock, we manage a full production-order workflow: technical specification, factory production, shipment planning, ocean transport, and destination delivery. You work with one accountable team from start to finish.
